What is "clutch"?

The NBA defines clutch time as the last 5 minutes of the 4th quarter (or overtime) when the score differential is 5 points or fewer. Those are the possessions that decide games — and reputations. This project pulls per-player clutch stats from the NBA Stats API, derives a few custom efficiency metrics, and turns it all into the dashboard above.
